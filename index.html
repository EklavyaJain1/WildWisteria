Our Story

A World of Flavours
in One Place

Nestled in Clement Town, Dehradun, Wild Wisteria is the city's most diverse dining destination — a café and restaurant where cuisines from across Asia, Europe, and the Americas come together under one roof.

From steaming Tibetan Thukpa and Korean Ramen to wood-fired Pizzas, fresh Pastas, zesty Mexican Enchiladas, and aromatic Pan Asian stir-fries — our 300+ dish menu has something for every craving and every occasion.

Whether you're here for a quiet coffee, a family dinner, or a celebratory feast — Wild Wisteria promises flavours that bloom long after the meal is over.
